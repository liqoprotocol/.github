# Liqo

**Global Payments Infrastructure for Modern Businesses**

Liqo enables businesses to accept payments from anywhere in the world, in any currency or payment method, and settle in the currency of their choice through a single API.

Our mission is to simplify global payments by abstracting payment providers, liquidity networks, blockchains, and settlement infrastructure behind a developer-friendly platform.

---

## Why Liqo?

Building global payment infrastructure is complex.

Businesses often need to integrate:

- Payment processors
- On/Off ramps
- Stablecoins
- Wallets
- Exchanges
- Liquidity providers
- FX providers
- Settlement networks

Liqo unifies these into a single platform so developers integrate once and scale globally.
